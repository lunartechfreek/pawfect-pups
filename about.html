About Us

Meet Our Trainers

Mark

Mark is our most experienced trainer having spent the last 20 years in the industry but his passion didn't begin there! Mark comes from a family of dog trainers, and spent his younger years travelling the country helping his parents run a variety of different competitions!

Beth

Beth is part of the furniture here and was with us from the start of our journey! She has 12 years experience in the industry but having grown up on a farm has always owned dogs amongst other animals. Beth is a self proclaimed animal lover of all shapes and sizes!

Benefits of training with us

No more chaotic walks

Is your pup isn't trying to drag you down the street to get to another dog? tugging on their leash? or trying to chase a squiral, after training with us your walks will be relaxing, enjoyable and stress free.

Stop bad behaviours

Are you tired of your dog destroying everything? stealing food off of your plate? Or running off with your underwear? After training with us you will find you and your dog are alot better room mates and create a bond you never imagined

Socialise with other dogs

Do you (and your dog) look over with envy at the dogs playing at the local dog park? Or maybe your just tired of them barking at every dog they see. Through social training cues and learning how to introduce dogs in the correct manner you and your pup can go join in the fun!

Leave them home alone

There is nothing worse than coming home and finding your dog has chewed the furniture, or maybe even actually destroyed your child's homework (the stories are true). After training with us you can leave your dog at home knowing that your house will still be intact

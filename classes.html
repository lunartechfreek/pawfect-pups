Classes

Our Selection of Classes

Group classes

Our group training is the most popular training we offer. Many people love this course because the biggest benefit is that you can help socialise your dog at the same time as training them!

One on one

This is our most effective course aimed for any age dog but It is particularly useful for adult dogs who maybe have behavioural problems or bad habits, as there are no distractions and they have the focus on them the entire time.

Online

For people who need flexibility in their training schedule and is our most budget friendly. Have our entire course at your finger tips from the moment you purchase. This will be permanently available for to get through as quickly as you want!

Agility

Are you and your dog ready to compete in the big leagues? Or maybe you just want a fun activity for you and your dog for some extra bonding time together? Regardless of the reason our agility course is the peak of the courses we offer!
